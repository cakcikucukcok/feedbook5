Tormented by the prolonged illness and tragic loss of his wife, Bill Allen, a weak, indecisive man, falls helplessly into a downward spiral of guilt and depression. His enraged cries for guidance and redemption are answered by his dead mother-in-law (Edith) on an inoperable phone stored in the attic. Their contentious relationship in life moves slowly to a forced alliance as Edith leads Bill through a maze of deception and murder. Their fiery telephone debates underpin this thriller and beg the question of life after, its substance, and our relationship to it!

The only way to find out where Edith can take you is by Speed Dialing the Dead!
