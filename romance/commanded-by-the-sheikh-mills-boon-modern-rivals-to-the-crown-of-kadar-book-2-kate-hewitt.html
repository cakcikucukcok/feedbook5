‘I can’t let the public know my bride is missing. I need someone else. You.’

To protect his throne, Sheikh Aziz al Bakir needs someone trustworthy to temporarily impersonate his missing fiancée. So the legendary Lothario of Europe demands that his housekeeper, Olivia Ellis, fulfil the role!

Olivia had thought Kadar was the perfect place to hide, but the Sheikh’s command leaves her open to global scrutiny. Even that would be easier to bear than his intense silver gaze! As ruthless as his desert ancestry, Aziz crashes through her reluctance and Olivia soon finds herself playing Queen in public… and lover in his bed!
