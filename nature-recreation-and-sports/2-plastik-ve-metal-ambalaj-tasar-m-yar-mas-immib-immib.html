2. Plastic and Metal Packaging Design Contest

In year 2015 Istanbul Chemicals and Chemical Products Exporters’ Association and Istanbul Ferrous and Non-Ferrous Exporters’ Association

coordinately organized the 2st Plastic and Metal Packaging Design Contest to the ends of developing high value-added plastics and metal

packaging products, spreading and promoting design activites, and boosting export-oriented competitive strength of the sector.

The contests has received 201 project applications in sum. A selective jury composed of top managers from the leading sectoral brands of Turkey

and abroad, industrialists, academicians and proffesional designers have meticulously evaluated the applicant projects and awarded 17 designs

in total.

We are delighted to share with you the promotional catalogue of the ranked projects in our 2015-dated contest.
