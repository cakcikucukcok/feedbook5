Have you ever been in the circle of a rainbow? Have you ever driven under three rainbows? Have you ever had a wolf smile at you? If you have pondered the true meaning of life, in these pages you will find answers from a fellow explorer who has discovered marvelous treasures on the path. Seen through Heathers eyes, the ordinary events of our life can become extraordinary opportunities filled with possibility.
