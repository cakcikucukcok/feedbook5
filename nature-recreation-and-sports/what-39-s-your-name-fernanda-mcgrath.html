Every pet should have a special name selected by their owner, just for them, that fits their personality and is unique to them. Whats Your Name?: Pets with Loving Names Know Love provides a list of possible names to help you find the most fitting name for your pet. Author Nan McGrath believes that keeping the name simple will make it easier on the owner when addressing their pet.

We sometimes find ourselves wondering how and why people give their pets the names that they do. There are really no rules or guidelines; its really a gut feeling, and you know when you have picked the right name. Some people know what they are going to name their pet before they even bring the pet home, while others take days or even weeks to choose the perfect name. Getting a pet is a happy time in our lives, and choosing the name can be just as much fun.

Arranged in alphabetical order, Whats Your Name offers hundreds of names for a variety of petsfrom Elmo to Jackson to Moose, the options are interesting and varied. You can find just the right name for your new pet.
