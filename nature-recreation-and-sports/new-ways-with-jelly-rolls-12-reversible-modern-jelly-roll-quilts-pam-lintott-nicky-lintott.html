Bestselling jelly roll quilt authors Pam and Nicky Lintott turn their expertise to modern designs for jelly roll strips.

The 12 stunning quilt designs are all accompanied by easy to follow step-by-step instructions which will you to get the quilts to come together very quickly for fast results.

Pam & Nicky also include a fabulous added bonus - as there is more negative space with modern quilt designs, they cleverly show you how to use the leftover strips from the front of your quilt to create beautiful quilt backs, which means the quilts are essentially reversible, so you get two for the price of one!
