A funkadelic trip to the not-so-distant past...

Disco, Smiley Faces, 8-tracks and platform shoes - retro is in and '70s rule! The Collectible '70s is a pop-culture history and price guide to treasures of this unforgettable decade. Covering everything from leisure suits to Pet Rocks, Saturday Night Fever to Punk Rock, this full-color guide will take you back to your fads, foibles and fashions of the polyester years.

This book is an essential reference for Baby Boomers and their younger siblings gathering the artifacts and memories of their youth.

Includes:

  • Hundreds of listings in over 20 categories
  • Up-to-date market prices
  • Informative and extremely entertaining background histories
