"This is the first novel to come along in a long time and deal with an international subject of true substance."-Kimberly Pruitt, Arlington, TX "A candid and mature work, Plea For Peace displays a heartfelt passion for human life and survival at all costs."-Dave A. Geary, Jackson, MS "Full of love and hardship, this novella is a premium collection of characters and situations that are worthy of sharing with the masses."-Deboraa Jordan, Chicago, IL "A generous dose of world hope that targets sexual expression in a glad-it's-fiction kind of way."-Redd Barron, Dallas, TX Plea For Peace chronicles the life and experiences of a woman who amasses commitments to abstinence from women throughout her community, state and country in an effort to end crime and wars. Women and men around the world eventually adopt the tactic for themselves. Fighting against their own desires for sexual fulfillment, the women eventually learn that holding out is easier said than done. They also experience major religious, political and business fallout that may cost them their mission, families and very lives.
