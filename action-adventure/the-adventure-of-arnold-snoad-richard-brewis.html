The diaryof Arnold Snoad was written as a daily chronicle of the adventures of Mr. Snoad. Together with his wife Elizabeth and his trusty canine companion Ranger, Arnold embarks on an adventure through London's dubious criminal underworld. Entirely unfased by the mishaps he encounters on the streets and in the clubs, Arnold's stoic character and unflinching wit carry him through the darkest of situations. How will he save the life of freedom he wants for himself and his wife? What risks will he need to endure, to escape to freedom? Will he avoid the inevitable fate of organised crime? Action packed and fast paced, the chronicles of Arnold Snoad are sure to set you on the edge of your seat. In this crime thriller, all rules are barred and anything goes... .
