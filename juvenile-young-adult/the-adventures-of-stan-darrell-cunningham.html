This is the first book in a series about a little blue car called STAN and his magical adventures with his friends- TOPPER the scarecrow, CHARLIE the crow, and two children BECKY & TOMAS. This book also introduces the childrens grandfather JAMES who has lovingly rebuilt STAN and his long suffering wife Emily who live on a farm. Part of the proceeds of this book will be donated to The Teenage Cancer Trust to help the charity with its important work. STAN is based on a rare 1939 Standard Flying Eight Tourer maintained by the Author and driven regularly in and around the New Forest Hampshire. The car has a web site www.standardflyingeight.co.uk Topper has his own web site www.topperthescarecrow.co.uk
