We sometimes loose our way so easily and silently becomes like a stone without thoughts, neither moving back or forth, wasting time, wasting life, without even knowing it. Simply because of one reason; not knowing what you truly need. You become like a tree without roots, holding on to nothing and reaching for nothing. Securing absolute and certain failure. Learn from the one thing that truly matters, the one thing that you cannot lie to, and the one thing that cannot lie to you; your heart. Let it teach you what truly matters to hold on to and what to reach for. Giving you a chance at what you truly need, in a world that is filled with so much deception, failure, and distress.
