Order in the House: Who's Leading You? is the first book in the series, Our Home Is

Protected by God. Finally! There's a book for married and single families from all different

ethnic groups and backgrounds!

       Many people believe there's no instruction manual for families.

Others believe the Bible is our instruction manual. If so, how do we go about applying the

Biblical principles to our lives?

      The author of Order in the House: Who's Leading You? shares true-to-life, detailed events

with the application of Biblical principles. She also poses questions to make the reader think,

“Who's leading me? Should I run my home in a certain way? Who decides how I should run my

home?”

The world has a leader for everything and an order of how things should run or operate.

Who decides the order and roles in our homes? Myself? The government? God?

       Felice answers these questions and more while sharing basic Biblical principles that can

be applied to every-day situations. She draws the reader in by sharing her experiences from her

own family. This book has many gems and tools to help the family grow. After reading this book,

you will know how to use basic Biblical principles in your every-day life.

This book also includes a note-taking section and a self-help journal (which can be found

at the end of the book). These are great tools for personal and family use. Let Him lead the way!
