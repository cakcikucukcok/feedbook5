Holding a name that exemplifies royal power and strength, Baron Hopgood is a native of Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. Born to Charles and Lura Hopgood, he was a young man destined for success. Baron began preaching at the tender age of three and went into full time ministry at twelve years old. Along with committing to God and fulfilling his purpose, Baron founded Baron Hopgood Enterprises.

Baron Hopgood is a preacher of the Gospel of Jesus Christ, a motivational and inspirational speaker, an accomplished and distinguished businessman, a devoted family man and has a passion for God. Baron Hopgood has survived the worst season of his life with a victory report to tell. This book chronicles his story from setbacks to comeback, and is guaranteed to inspire anyone going through a season of storms. The message is simple and profound: there is nothing you can experience in this life that you can't make a Comeback from. Your story is not over, and one day it will be told.

With that being said, Baron Hopgood is proven, tested, tried, redeemed, restored and still standing. His insights will become your steps to ultimate victory.
