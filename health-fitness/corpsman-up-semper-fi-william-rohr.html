            It’s difficult for Corpsman Ben Carpenter to watch his fellow Marines – or the Viet Cong – suffer and die during his first few months in Vietnam. He gets by with the help of Red, his best friend and fellow corpsman, and his unwavering faith in God.

            Operation Colorado, a multi-battalion search and destroy operation, aims to rid the Tam Ky area of a Viet Cong stronghold. It brings Ben and his friends closer to the front lines, and to danger.

            As Operation Colorado’s casualties mount, Ben witnesses war atrocities he never could have imagined back in California. All the while, Hill 130, a place that will challenge everything Ben knows about the world, looms closer.

            Written by a decorated Vietnam War corpsman. Corpsman Up! Semper Fi is a moving story of war, friendship, and faith.
