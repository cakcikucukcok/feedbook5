Since her publication of Pursuit of Purpose, Adel Roy has been examining the role that we play in the lives of others.  In her latest work, Making the Connector, she takes her readers on a journey of personal revelation and discovery.  Exploring stories from her childhood,her adulthood, her role as a parent and the world around her.  Adel finds meaning in the small, often overlooked interactions connecting them to our spiritual and personal growth.  Sometimes life presents challenges that tempt us to resist God's instruction.  Making the Connector provides relatable accounts of everyday troubles as well as faith shaking life events that guided Adel to this point in her life.  Follow her journey and rise to meet your role as a connector in the lives of those around you.
