'Poems Everyone Enjoys' is an anthology of 50 original poems, by the author. It's for anyone of mature age (16+), including non-traditional poetry readers. In this ebook second edition each poem is supported by a black / white illustration. The vast majority of the poems were inspired by true events - consisting of his observation of others, of life in general and of London's natural and physical environment. It also includes some personal experiences and opinions of the author. There is also a large 'Personalised Poems' section, which makes a riveting read. This ebook should, therefore, contain poems that will appeal to any reader, in some form or another. The author's poems on people are insightful, on humour he shows wit and on places in his environment his observations are stunningly descriptive. To further facilitate the reader's enjoyment of this ebook the author has included a glossary. This should be invaluable to many readers, especially if English is not their first language.
