'Poems Everyone Enjoys' is an anthology of 50 poems written by the author. It's for anyone of mature age (16+), including non-traditional readers of poetry. The vast majority of the poems were inspired by true events - consisting of the author's observation of others, of life in general and of London's natural and physical environment. It also includes some personal experiences and opinions of the author. A large 'Personalised Poetry' section is included which readers will find entertaining as well. This ebook should, therefore, contain poems that will appeal to any reader, in some form or another. The author's poems on people are insightful, on humour he shows wit and on places in his environment his observations are stunningly descriptive. To further facilitate the reader's enjoyment of this ebook the author has included a glossary. This should be invaluable to many readers, especially if English is not their first language.
