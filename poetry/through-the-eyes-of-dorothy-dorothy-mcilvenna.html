She wrote 3 novels, many childrens stories, drew her own illustrations, wrote music lyrics, one hymn and lots of poetry. She wrote two stories for young teenagers that she published herself. Her legacy was for her books to be published for her family. After she passed it was decided to have her books published for the world to enjoy. This book is her book of Poetry, song lyrics, skits and essays. She also wrote childrens stories, 3 Novels (Stamp of Approval, Peters Plight, Time will Tell) and two teen books (The Secret Cave, The Runaway). She was a very loving mother who said her Rosary daily helping her family thru many trials on her knees in prayer. She is now with the Lord but her legacy thru her books and family will live on forever.
