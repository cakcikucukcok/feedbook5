We all have a desire to live a peaceful and meaningful life. We struggle through trials and tribulations. Sometimes the struggle of lifes adversities makes it difficult to find that inner peace we desire. You may be struggling with the lost of a loved one; a rebellious child, and abusive relationship; a demanding job, financial burdens; health issues, addictions, etc. Only you can make a decision to defy the demons that are preventing you from finding your inner peace. When you learn to let go and let God; only then will you receive the inner peace you desire. I pray that this book of poems will help strengthen your faith and move you closer to the one that can give you Your Peace Within
