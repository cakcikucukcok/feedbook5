Ive never professed to be a great writer, but over the years, my talent as a writer and a poet has been fashioned and molded far beyond my wildest dreams. Ive always been a private person, from birth, and sharing my work with the world was never a real thought until a few years ago. Thats when I learned that my writing impacted more lives that I thought, so here we are, years later, with the finished product, I am very proud of my accomplishment and of my work. I write a lot, and theres plenty more where soulful eyes came from. I write from the heart and soul, with every line I strive to let my true feelings and emotions show. At the end of every poem, I am pleased, and I try to convey that same positive spirit to my readers.
