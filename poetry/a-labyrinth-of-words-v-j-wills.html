An interplay of ideas, a collaboration of experiences, a pool of thought, and A Labyrinth of Words. Authored by V. J. Wills, this book presents a selection of poetry written with uncommon poise. Here, Wills presents her writing prowess as she attempts to capture the ramblings of the human mind and the downpour of raw emotions in verses that can only be described as unique and innovative. You will also discover a different style of writing in this collection; one that is raw and unguarded, profound at the same time. Dwell in A Labyrinth of Words and appreciate once more the beauty of expression.
