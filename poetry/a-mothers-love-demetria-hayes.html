“A Mother’s Love” is informative and educational. A noteworthy feature in the book is that it contains a guide to how a mother can engage and earn the trust of her children. It is a collaboration of individual expressions of a mother’s love through diverse perspectives from children and mothers.

 “Mothers suffer much, endure much, but still find a way to change the world… One day at a time,” said the author who was a finalist for the prestigious Phillis Wheatley Book Award.

“A Mother’s Love’’ also contains practical suggestions to assist young women with the challenges of motherhood. Getting this book will not be an option but a necessity for any mother or would be mother.

On this refreshing read of ‘’A Mother’s Love,’’ you will hear heartfelt letters, poems, and quotes that reflect the love of a mother and child.

It is a treasure to have this insightful book on every family bookshelf as it is a handy companion to promote and create a healthy bond between a mother and her children.
