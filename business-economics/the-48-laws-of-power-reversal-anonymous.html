By now, the business world is probably familiar with Robert Greene's best-selling book, The 48 Laws of Power. Using excerpts from history, film, popular culture, and famous villains, the expanded edition of The 48 Laws of Power Reversal will be about 500 pages in length, much like the Robert Greene book readers are familiar with.
