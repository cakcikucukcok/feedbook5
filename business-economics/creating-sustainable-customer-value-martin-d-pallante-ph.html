Creating Sustainable Customer Value...The Positive Power of Strategic Management" is the culmination of 40 years of experience leading people and managing enterprises and projects. The author, Dr. Martin D. Pallante, spent more than 40 years in senior positions of sales, marketing and general management eventually rising to the position of President and CEO of a large, multi-national division of a New York Stock Exchange company. Pallante's division, with plants locatedacross the globe, produced essential products and services for the plastics, rubber, metals, auto,glass and carbonated beverage industries. During his illustrious career, Pallante encountered many opportunities and challenges that not only tested his management and leadership capabilities but allowed him to develop a unique approach to management and leadership that is the centerpiece of this new strategic management initiative. Creating Sustainable Customer Value is a concise, direct to the point, step-by-step primerthat iseasily understood.
