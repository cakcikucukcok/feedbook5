Volume three. The last book of the trilogy follows my lifetime of love, sex and relationships from loneliness to finding the man of my dreams. My book is explicit and for adults only. I hope you will find it fun and learn some new sexual techniques to try. If nothing else, this book proves that true love will always find a way through the tangled web of life, sex and relationships.
