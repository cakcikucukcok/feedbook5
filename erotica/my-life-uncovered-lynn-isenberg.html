Laura Taylor, an ambitious heart centered naive Midwestern English major is determined to go west and write the great American screenplay; an epic love story. Working her way through Hollywood's premiere talent agency, she gets close to making her dreams come true. But when money gets tight she takes a writing job at a small independent film company only to inadvertently discover she's written the great American adult movie. Laura is soon juggling two identities between two worlds in this hilarious expose of Hollywood meets the Adult Film Industry as she attempts to remain true to her integrity. Get ready for a hot steamy ride of double identities and sister industries as Laura finds her true self and true love in this wonderful fish out of water tale a la Tootsie.
