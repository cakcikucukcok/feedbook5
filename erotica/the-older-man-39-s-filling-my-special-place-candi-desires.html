"Wow," Oliver says with a smile, shaking his head. "I can't believe you're finally an adult now."

 

Karina, Oliver's 18-year old friend's daughter, is having her birthday today. She smiles brightly at her buddy.

 

"I know," she says. "It's hard for me to believe too."

 

"So Oliver," Karina waggles her eyebrows at him. "What say you pick us up some beer and let's get this party started, huh?"

 

Oliver gapes at her, and Karina can only hold in her laughter for so long.

 

"Ha ha, I'm sorry Oliver," she says. "I couldn't help it. I know I still can't legally drink yet."

 

Oliver can't help but chuckle at his friend's daughter's joke.

 

"That's okay, honey," he says with a smile. "I knew you were messing around."

 

Karina is getting close to getting her period, but she's not there yet. This is the time of her cycle where she's the most fertile. It's also the time where she feels the most turned on.

 

Several times a day over the past week Karina's had to excuse herself to go relieve a little tension, but today she doesn't think her hand is going to do it. Her brain has cottoned on. She knows that she needs something else. Her body is craving a real person. And her body is craving for her to become pregnant.

 

"You know what, Oliver?" Karina says, and she steps forward, wrapping her arms around Oliver, giving him a hug.

 

Oliver is shocked for a moment, but then he hugs his little princess back. When they separate he says, "What was that for?"

 

"For taking care of all of this," Karina tells him. "And just for…for being the best friend a girl could ask for."

 

She smiles up into his eyes, and he smiles right back down at her.

 

"You know, honey," Oliver says to his friend's daughter. "I must say, I get worried about you at your college sometimes."

 

"Oh yeah?" Karina asks. "Why do you get worried?"

 

"Because, all the guys there must be falling over themselves trying to get to you," he says. Karina looks up at him as he takes in her incredible body and beautiful face with one look. "And I must say, if I were there, I'd do the exact same thing."
