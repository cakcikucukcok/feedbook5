This book is about the humanity of love. When Brianna Miller meets Isaac Hayes, they know that their love will last forever, but soon Isaac has to make a choicestay and endanger her or leave in an effort to protect her. Brianna too has to make a choicelive the remainder of her life in dreams together with Isaac or learn to live a life of reality without him. Brianna soon finds herself beckoned to breathe life again for the sake of another man, one whose dark, morbid, manipulative, and secret past ensnares and entangles her in a manner in which she cannot escape. Dominick Waul, a prominent wealthy psychiatrist has fallen in love with beautiful Brianna. He is in love with the manner in which Brianna is capable of loving. Dominick vows to take the love unto him by any means necessary. Dominick uses hypnosis on his unsuspecting patient in an effort to draw her to him. This book is filled with explosive sensual and erotic love scenes and nuances that wrap around your mind and swaddle it like a baby in a blanket. Its filled with good people who do bad things for loves sake and will have you asking yourself, What manner of love is this?
