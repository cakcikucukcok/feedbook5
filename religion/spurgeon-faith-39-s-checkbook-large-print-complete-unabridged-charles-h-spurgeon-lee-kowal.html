SURGEON - FAITH'S CHECKBOOK - LARGE PRINT - (COMPLETE  & UNABRIDGED): 365 Days of Encouragement. Charles Spurgeon was one of the most dearly beloved Christian preachers and teachers of all time!  Why has Charles Spurgeon touched the hearts of so many over the past 100 years?  His manner of delivering Christ’s golden Scripture was poetical, gentle, and with utmost humility – it captures the heart and soul in a manner that reveals God to us in a real way!   

Surgeon could find an apple of golden wisdom from any verse in the Bible and somehow present it to us such that we also see the treasure before us.  In this way he reaped endless amounts of ‘spiritual manna’ to feed us — the sheep of our Great Shepherd. 

Faith’s Checkbook is unique from Spurgeon’s other devotionals in that it it’s focus is primarily comfort and encouragement…  to help us see and feel the sweetness of our Savior with warmth and joy.  The reader is also drawn to His truth.

Surgeon took no credit for any of his success, but attributed the fruit of his sermons and devotionals only to God Himself.  In fact, he prayed of this very journal, “May many readers, whom I may never see, discover the duty and delight of such child-like trust in God while they are reading the little bit which I have prepared for each day in the year….  May our Lord Jesus accept this my service for His sheep and lambs.”

It is our pleasure to present Spurgeon’s Faith’s Checkbook Large Print, an unabridged edition –May it bless those who may not be unable to read it otherwise.  Our prayer is that it’s readers will be blessed to know better their Savior – Growing ever more closer to Him.
