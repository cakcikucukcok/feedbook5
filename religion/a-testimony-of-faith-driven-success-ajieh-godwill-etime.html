This is a true-life testimony of what success can be attained if you have a purpose in life, a defined goal that pushes you never to give up even under such conditions considered as impossible, not letting situations to define your destiny but instead the other way round. The narrations in this book are another amazing testimony to the world at large that God truly lives and we all can harvest the fruits of the curriculum vitae he has already prepared for us. If you think you have gone through difficulties that have no solution or are blocked by the challenges that come your way, then here comes your solution. This book guides you on how to overcome challenges that stand to impede your progress in life. This is a story of a child from a peasant home in a hopeless environment presented with all sorts of human and natural challenges but who refuses to succumb to them with determination, humility, focus and Gods favor, becoming a reference in the world today in his career and education.
