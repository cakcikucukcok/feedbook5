More astonishing is shes an employee of Eastern Bank, also is his uncles secretary. His heart turns over and quickly in love after knowing her name is Ngan. Getting closer to Ngan and trying to win her heart, Brian intrigues with his uncle to take a mailman job instead to disguise his character. And from there, they became more than just colleagues, but as so often happens in times of need friendships are forged, and after Brian is acting as an actor to pretend Ngans beau to visit her family, and then secretly helps Ngan to rework her project.
