Understanding Your Dreams is a Christian educative book, which tries to explain the subject dream as a spiritual entity in a human being. It brings into light the various misconceptions people have about dreams based on the backgrounds they speak from. This book also outlines the importance of dreams to mankind. The book defines dream in the writers perspective based on the Bible and insight based on experience, revelation, and divine inspiration. It also categorizes dreams into three, depending on the way they happen and the characters involved. It further provides both scriptural and practical examples of the various types of dreams for easy and complete understanding. Again, this book looks at the various steps involved in dream interpretation, and finally, it has a list of some common symbols in our modern dreams and their probable meaning.
