Devil women – all of them! The sultry and sophisticated Elizabeth Burnblack, along with her sexy coven of witches, has made a pact with the Devil–specifically, the high ranking demon Lucifuge Rofocale–for fame and fortune. They are a satanic hedonist cult of devil worshippers living in present-day London, casting spells and practicing the blackest of all magic. To make matters worse, they do not hide their witchcraft, while their sensual desires go beyond all erotic limits! What does the cannibalistic dwarf Ryan Lowry know of their crimes? Can the Freemasons and Illuminati stop their murderous escapades? And what does the spirit of someone who died on September 11, 2001, have to say about all this?

Following in the footsteps of Sir Author Conan Doyle (“Sherlock Holmes”), D.H. Lawrence (Lady Chatterley's Lover), the Marquis de Sade (Justine, or the Misfortunes of Virtue), Aleister Crowley (Moonchild), Dennis Wheatley (The Devil Rides Out), Ira Levin (Rosemary’s Baby), Clive Barker (Hellraiser), and Umberto Eco (The Name of the Rose, Foucault’s Pendulum), Robert W. Sullivan IV weaves a Satanic murder-mystery of unparalleled deliciously evil proportions. A Pact with the Devil is an erotic crime supernatural thriller not to be read by the faint of heart.
