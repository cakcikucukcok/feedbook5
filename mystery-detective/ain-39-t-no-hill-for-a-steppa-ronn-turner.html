This story begins in 1961, in a place not considered very significant, Phoenix Arizona. It was wide open to numerous factions vying for superiority. Two Afro American men, during a time of national unrest, rose to be Robin Hoods of the community and opened a night club called the "El Morrocco". To avoid becoming the focal point for some viscous murders, they had to solve them, or lose everything. They were feared and loved men in a city with little rules. Mixed with laughter, crazy characters and a strong dose of "who did it?" and you'll know why these men would always say..."Ain't no hill fo a Steppa".
