The Longest Journey offers nine keys which anyone can follow for health wealth and happiness. Whether you are in debt, experiencing chemical dependency, homeless, lonely, depressed - or simply want to be happier and healthier, this book can change your life for the better. Sandy takes you through the nine keys one at a time using his own life experiences to illustrate how each key is applied. This is not a book on theory but one of proven practicality. This book will change your life - no question - from fear and doubt to one of joy and hope.

Prepare yourself for a journey of a lifetime, the longest journey of all, a journey into the self.

Together we will go through each key and start the process of unlocking the barriers to personal fulfilment. To achieving a life of peace, love and joy, that I believe is your God given right to experience.

No matter how bleak your life may be at this moment, I promise you that reading this book and working the 9 keys will transform your life. I can make this promise because I am living proof that the 9 keys work. I have experienced homelessness, addiction, prison, despair, fear, failed relationships, suicide attempts, spiritual bankruptcy and financial bankruptcy. I use my experiences in this book in order to encourage YOU to start taken control of your life and you can.

Your life is about to change big time I promise you.
