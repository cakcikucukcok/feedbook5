Angels by the Roadside represents a compilation of the greatest and most memorable moments in Doras life in which God sent angels to, at times, deliver a message or guide her in the making of an important decision. At other times the angels even protected her against imminent danger. Her recognition of those divine helpers as angels was sometimes immediate and other times the product of much contemplation and prayer. There is little doubt that this book will cause the reader to ask, Could that unexpected helper in my life also have been an angel? This book will inspire the reader to rely on Gods provision and realize that sometimes the most seemingly coincidental occurrences in life may actually be an angelic visitation gone unnoticed.
