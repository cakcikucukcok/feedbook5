Richard has been a security system salesman for about a year now. He's good at what he does and loves his job, but doesn't feel valued at the workplace. He's passed up for promotions he feels he should get and is constantly second-guessed. His anger starts to affect his job performance. Stressed out Richard goes to a bar to try to calm his nerves. On his way home from the bar he is waved down by Nautilus who is nothing but trouble. Nautilus is a seemingly normal guy who just so happens to be an alien. Nautilus believes that Richard is the "special one." The one destined to rule his planet, but everything's not as it seems and Richard has to find that out the hard way. Or does he?
