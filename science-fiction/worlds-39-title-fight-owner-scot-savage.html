"Nemo Regit Mea Vita" is a Latin phrase that means "No one controls my destiny." That is the motto of Rodrigo "Peppy (Pepe)" Velez, a proud Mexican-American boxer and current undefeated seven-time world champion. Just when he is on the verge of retirement so he can live a quiet life with the woman he loves, he is faced with one more challenge in the form of Fighter X the Ultimate... a welterweight champion from another world! Peppy doesn't realize that there is more at stake than just his title belt and his undefeated streak. If he losses, another alien race will suffer for it! Can Peppy muster up all his strength and courage to meet the alien challenger... and is he willing to pay the ultimate price for victory?
