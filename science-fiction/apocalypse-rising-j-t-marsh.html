Apocalypse Rising is a novel about a working class revolution in near-future Britain. Fifteen years ago, a working class revolution swept across Europe. In Britain, Valeri Kovalenko’s mother and father died taking part, their revolution failing, but their deaths not to be in vain. Now, Valeri and the working men of Britain face a crisis worse than anyone before them, with the rich freely looting and plundering the country’s wealth while the people live under the oppression of unemployment, violence, and death. But not all is lost. Pushed to the brink of starvation, Valeri finally realizes what must be done. But there are greater forces at work, a titanic battle between good and evil to see prophets and demons, heroes and villains, the all-powerful face off against the hopelessly corrupt and sinful in the beginnings of a war that'll change all our lives, forever.

The first in a trilogy of novels, Apocalypse Rising is equal parts prophecy and premise, with a gripping story and formidable characters making for a compelling must-read.
