Each quote or saying in this book was penned with a story of inspiration behind it but this knowledge is only known to the author. However, you will see that some of the entries have thoughts to process following them in brackets. This book has a sorted collection of 101 quotations and sayings with the final quotation given under the title My Gift to You. On this page a quote is listed and a full explanation detailing the mindset of the author is disclosed.
