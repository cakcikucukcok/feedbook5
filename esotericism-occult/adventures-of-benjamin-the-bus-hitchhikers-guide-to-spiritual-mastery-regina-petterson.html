Nostalgic for the innocent days of peace and love, and still scented with the patchouli oil of his youth, sweet Benjamin, a 1966 21-Window Volkswagen bus, impatiently waits for his family to leave town so he can sneak out of his garage, and head towards the beach. Unaware that he’s embarking on the grooviest adventure of his life, he cruises along his favorite stretch of Pacific Coast Highway, carved out by a winding, majestic tapestry of mountains and shoreline. One after the other, three hitchhikers appear along the highway, each bestowing his own special wisdom on Benji as they travel together on a magical journey. His passengers are no ordinary mortals—all are ascended masters from the highest spiritual dimension—Michael Jackson, Jesus, and Buddha. They come with profound teachings that transform Benji’s limiting beliefs and low self-esteem, and ignite his belief in his unlimited potential to be a master himself. As the miles roll by underneath them, Benjamin The Bus joyfully awakens to his true divine purpose in life.
