Lenore by Edgar Allan Poe | Lenore by Edgar Allan Poe. ‘Lenore’ by Edgar Allan Poe was first published in 1831 under the title ‘A Pæan.’. It was at that time an eleven quatrain poem that was told from the perspective of a bereaved husband mourning the death of his wife. It was not until the re-publication in February of 1843 that the name “Lenore” was included in the text.
