Though The Nightstalker Saga: Origins is his first step into high fantasy, Abe manages to create a well composed and compelling story following Mark Williamson and his brother Bill as they adventure through the Überrealms. Excellent character development, in-depth lore, and a one-of-a-kind language truly transport the reader to another place, time, and universe. After starting the book it's difficult to tear yourself away, and the reader often ends up waiting eagerly for the next big twist. Anyone who enjoyed the Lord of the Rings or The Wheel of Time should keep an eye out for this series. Abe Winterscheidt creates a one of a kind, immense universe and lore in his first delve into Epic Fantasy. Powerful, compelling, and expansive, The Nightstalker Saga: Origins introduces the reader into a world set to become the next Middle Earth. Abe Winterscheidt is a university student at the College of William and Mary, an author, and photographer (check him out at: www.imaginethesky.com).
