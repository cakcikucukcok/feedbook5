Nightmares aren't merely figments of our imaginations. They're real, living beings that spawn from fear in Dreamscapes while we're asleep. Without Sanya and Reve these creatures would flood into our world and become a reality. They're young, but they form a strong and deadly team. This team has been paired together for a shocking reason. They're experiments created by the organization they work for to defeat the Nightmares they're slowly losing to. Will these green-eyed Nightmare executioners pull through? Or will their quickly growing feelings for each other tear their hearts and minds in two?
