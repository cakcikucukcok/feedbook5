What matters in life is an individual’s response to reality...

 

Beyond Oblivion follows Donna as she unwittingly becomes a whistleblower. As a result, she discovers the mystery behind her friend Carrie’s life and how this secret places her friend at the center of a global conspiracy.

 

Donna should just be wrapping up her graduate degree at the university, yet due to a series of events she fails to foresee, her degree is threatened, and several former allies begin to turn against her. Before she knows it, she stumbles upon an illicit act that must be called to public attention. Her whistleblower act will unexpectedly take her into Carrie’s past. Donna realizes that there is a truth behind Carrie’s dreams that must be uncovered before it is too late. Following up on the clues Carrie uncovers, Donna finds herself forced to make a run for it in a world where there are no rules. An unwitting whistleblower, she connects with several people who help her unravel the mystery affecting Carrie’s life and understand the connection it has to a web of startling international intrigue.

 

In the process, she recovers her generation while finding the love and strength to go beyond the given information and transform.
