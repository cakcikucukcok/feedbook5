Sid is an investment Banker and currently the Vice president of Ascii(97-122) Solutions. He is pitched against Yash for the soon to be vacant position of the Managing director. The latter is elevated to the post and has a fall out with Sid who quits his job, breaks up with his girlfriend and meets with an accident. Here Sid happens to encounter a mysterious stranger whom he calls as Dhootji. Over a couple of meetings Dhootji offers him 3 boons of his choice for which he would take away something that would benefit the entire mankind. It begins as an innocent game but what follows is a dangerous roller coaster ride of 3 Boons give and take that changes the face of India significantly.
