Elith Elidove was once a commoner, maid, and aspiring young musician. She played in a band with her parents and boyfriend for years, building their reputation and credibility as professional entertainers. Elith & her banded turned into celebrities after a strange encounter with a peculiar halfing who enchanted her most important possession of all, her lute. The hafling promised she will return the favor to him, one day... Later Elith struggles on her own after a bitter conflict and loss. After many close calls forcing her to learn from her own weaknesses, see wises up, finds new friends, and seeks justice for a wrong done to her and her family.
