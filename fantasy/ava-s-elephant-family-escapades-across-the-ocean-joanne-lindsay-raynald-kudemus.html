Ava's Elephant Family Escapades is a series of adventures experienced by four royal elephants who left Africa with a woman named Grammy. The first book showed how the baby elephants were chosen to be royal elephants and how they obtained their colorful and bejeweled saddles. This second book shows their journey across the ocean in a ship heading toward their new life in America. There, they will meet Princess Ava in a town called Palmetto and finally see the castle they will live in. Look for the third story, where the elephants embark on adventures in their new home.
