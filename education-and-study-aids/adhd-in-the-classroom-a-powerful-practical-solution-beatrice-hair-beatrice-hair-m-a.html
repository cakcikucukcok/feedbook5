ADHD in the Classroom: A Powerful, Practical Solution is a book about a tool designed for the classroom teachers to help their ADHD students reach their potential. This book can also be used with any student or at home. What do ADHD students need? They crave structure, consistency,and individual attention. This workbook is designed to equip teachers with tools to create powerful solutions for ADHD. Not only does this tool command students attention, it helps keep them motivated.

Heartiest congratulations to Beatrice Hair on the publication of her first book. Parents and teachers, and most importantly, students with ADHD, will all benefit from the experience and expertise she shares in this book.

Senator Elizabeth Dole
